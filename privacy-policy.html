1. Introduction

Welcome to Picsora (the “App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By installing and using Picsora, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Picsora is designed to play videos, music, show photos, and provide editing features.

We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. However, the following information may be accessed locally on your device:

  • Media files (photos, videos, audio) stored on your device.
  • App usage data (basic crash reports and performance data via Google Play Services).

3. How We Use Information

The information accessed by Picsora is used only to:

  • Play your videos, music, and display photos.
  • Allow you to edit your media files.
  • Improve app stability and fix bugs (through anonymous crash logs).

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

4. Permissions Used

Picsora may request the following permissions:

  • Storage / Media Access: To access and play your videos, music, and photos.
  • Network Access (Optional): For checking updates, ads, or analytics (if applicable).

5. Third-Party Services

Picsora may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Services (for app functionality and updates).
  • Ad Networks (if monetized, e.g., AdMob) to display ads.

These third parties may collect anonymized information according to their own policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

Picsora is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

Your media files remain on your device. We do not upload or store your files on external servers.

Standard security practices are applied to protect app usage data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected with a new “Last Updated” date.
